About Me

I’m a Computer Science student at Elizabethtown College with a concentration in Cyber Security. I’m originally from Lancaster Pennsylvania, and I’ve developed a strong interest in building software systems.

Through my coursework and projects, I’ve gained experience in Java, SQL, C, and Python, focusing on object-oriented programming, data structures, and algorithm design. I’ve built projects such as a security log analyzer, a predictive data model, and recursive problem-solving tools, which have strengthened my ability to analyze problems and develop practical solutions.

I’m particularly interested in cybersecurity, data analysis, and backend development, and I’m always looking for opportunities to apply my skills in real-world environments and continue learning.
